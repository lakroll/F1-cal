Privacy Policy

F1 Calendar — Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. Overview

F1 Calendar ("the app") is a Formula 1 race schedule application developed for personal use and made available on the App Store and Google Play. This policy explains how the app handles information.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No crash reporting services
  • No third-party advertising SDKs

3. Calendar Permission

The app requests access to your device's calendar solely to add Formula 1 session events (practice, qualifying, race) when you explicitly tap "Add to Calendar". The app writes events to your local calendar and does not read, copy, or transmit any existing calendar data.

4. Notification Permission

The app may request permission to send local notifications as reminders for upcoming race sessions. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and are not sent through any external server. No notification content or delivery data leaves your device.

5. External Data Sources

Race schedule data is retrieved from Jolpica (api.jolpi.ca), a public open-source mirror of the former Ergast Motor Racing API. Driver and constructor standings data is retrieved from OpenF1 (api.openf1.org), a free and open-source real-time Formula 1 data API. No personal data is sent to these services — only standard HTTP requests to fetch public race data.

6. Data Storage

The app stores your language preference locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no personal data from anyone, it is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact: lakroll.corp@gmail.com
